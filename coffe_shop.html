ABOUT ME

Welcome To Cocopie! a cafe that offers the perfect blend of high-quality coffee and authentic Japanese cuisine! At Cocopie, you can enjoy a variety of lovingly brewed coffee while savoring traditional Japanese dishes such as ramen, yakitori, and donburi. We create a cozy and warm atmosphere, ideal for relaxing or gathering with friends. Our friendly team is ready to provide the best service for an unforgettable experience. Come visit Cocopie and experience the deliciousness of coffee and Japanese cuisine all in one place!
